Every believer was created by God to succeed. No particular person was created for success. In the first book of the bible, we learn that the first mandate God gave to man was for man to be successful. So God created man in His own image, in the image of God created he him; male and female created he them. And God blessed them, and God said unto them, Be fruitful, and multiply… Genesis 1:27-28 KJV Firstly, God created mankind in His image and likeness. God’s image and likeness has to do with the way God behaves. In other words, God put what makes Him God into man. Also, man had no hand in his creation as to how he was created.
